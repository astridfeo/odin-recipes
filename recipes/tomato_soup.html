Tomato soup

Classic tomato soup

An essential every home cook needs? This classic Tomato Soup recipe! There’s nothing more homey than a big pot bubbling on the stove with its bright garlicky aroma. Add to that a browned, gooey grilled cheese from the griddle and well: it’s basic in all the right ways. Just a like a classic Caesar salad or avocado toast: a great tomato soup is not rocket science but always, always satisfying. This recipe is beyond easy to make and will please everyone in the family (even the picky ones).

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Canned tomatoes: fire roasted if possible
  • Vegetable broth
  • Garlic powder and onion powder
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Optional: basil, heavy cream

Steps

  1. Mince the garlic, then sauté in olive oil for 30 seconds.
  2. Add the canned tomatoes, broth onion powder, garlic powder, and salt. Simmer 15 to 20 minutes.
  3. Immersion blend or carefully blend the soup
  4. Add butter and salt to taste. Voila!
  5. OptionalAdd ½ cup chopped basil while simmering. This adds the best tomato basil flavor!
  6. OptionalAdd a drizzle of heavy cream to finish. This incorporates a delightful lightly sweet richness and warmth to the texture.
